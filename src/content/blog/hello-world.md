Welcome to my blog! I'm Shubham Goel, a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Meta working on Instagram ads ranking.

## What to expect

I plan to write about things I find interesting and useful:

- **Machine Learning** — practical insights from building ML systems at scale
- **Multimodal LLMs** — experiments and learnings with models like Gemini, GPT-4V, and Claude
- **AI Agents** — building systems that can reason, plan, and take actions
- **Side Projects** — walkthroughs of things I build in my spare time
- **Computer Vision** — my background area, from satellite imagery to skin cancer detection

## A bit about me

I've spent the last several years working across the ML stack — from building footprint extraction at Pitney Bowes and Reliance Jio, to hurricane damage estimation at UIUC, to multimodal search systems at Zefr, and now ads ranking at Meta.

I hold an M.S. in Statistics from UIUC and a B.Tech from the University of Delhi.

Outside of work, I'm interested in startups, education, philosophy, and football. I firmly believe that *specialization is for insects*.

Stay tuned for more posts!
